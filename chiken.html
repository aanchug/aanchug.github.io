Butter Chicken Recipe

Ingredients

50gm butter, 1kg chiken, 50gm plain flour, one chopped onion, 10gm peas, red chilly, salt

Cooking mechanism

  1. Please take the plain flour and mix well with some water
  2. Deep fry the onpions and chilly
  3. Fill the stuff in the puff pastry
  4. Serve buttery chicken curry hot
